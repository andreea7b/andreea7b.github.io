Amirhossein Noohian

Hi! My name is Amirhossein Noohian. I have an M.Sc. degree in Mechanical Engineering from Sharif University of Technology (SUT), Tehran, Iran. For my master's thesis, I conducted research on reinforcement learning and learning from demonstration in robotics.
